Frequently Asked Questions

Is LEX IQ completely free?

Yes. LEX IQ is free to use. There are no paid plans, no premium tiers, and no paywalls. All quizzes, leaderboards, and live rooms are accessible to everyone.

How do I sign in?

Click the "Sign In" button on the homepage and use your Google account. No password is required — Google handles authentication securely.

My score didn't save. What do I do?

Make sure you are signed in before starting a quiz. Scores only save for signed-in users. If you were signed in and the score still didn't save, it may be a temporary connection issue — try again or contact us.

How do I host a Live Room for my class?

Click "Live Room" in the navigation, sign in, select a Part and difficulty, and create a room. Share the 6-digit room code with your students. They join at the same URL using the code.

I found an error in a question. How do I report it?

DM us on Instagram @lexiqquiz with the Part, the question text, and the correct answer with the relevant Article reference. We review and correct all reported errors.

How do I delete my account?

Sign in → click your profile picture → Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account, scores, and all data from our database.

When will Criminal Law / IPC quizzes be available?

We're actively working on it. Criminal Law is the next section on our roadmap. Follow @lexiqquiz to get notified when it launches.
