Privacy Policy

📅 Effective: March 2026 📍 Applicable to lexiqquiz.github.io/lexiq

LEX IQ ("we", "our", "the platform") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using LEX IQ, you agree to the practices described here.

📌 We only collect what is necessary to provide the quiz experience. We do not sell your data to anyone.

01 Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive and store:

  • Your full name and email address — used to identify your account
  • Your Google profile picture — displayed on the leaderboard and your profile
  • Your Google User ID — used as a unique account identifier

When you take a quiz, we record:

  • Your score, time taken, and difficulty level for each quiz attempt
  • The Constitution Part you attempted
  • Timestamp of submission

We do not collect passwords, phone numbers, location data, or payment information.

02 How We Use Your Information

  • To display your name and picture on the live leaderboard (if you have enabled this)
  • To show you your own quiz history, scores, and performance analytics
  • To calculate your average score and best rank in your profile
  • To maintain your session across devices via localStorage
  • To improve quiz quality and identify which sections need more questions

03 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase, a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS infrastructure in the Asia-Pacific (Singapore) region. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

Row Level Security (RLS) is enabled on all database tables, meaning your data is protected by database-level access rules — not just application-level logic.

🔒 We never store your Google password. Authentication is handled entirely by Google's secure OAuth 2.0 system.

04 Third-Party Services

LEX IQ uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Sign-In (OAuth 2.0) — for authentication only
  • Supabase — database for scores, users, and questions
  • Firebase Realtime Database — for live room functionality only
  • GitHub Pages — for hosting the static website
  • Google Fonts — for typography (no personal data shared)

05 Your Privacy Controls

You have full control over your data. From your Profile (click your avatar after signing in) you can:

  • Toggle whether your name appears on the public leaderboard
  • Enable or disable quiz history saving
  • Toggle analytics tracking
  • Permanently delete your account and all associated data

06 Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies. We use your browser's localStorage solely to maintain your login session across page refreshes — this data never leaves your device except during explicit API calls.

07 Children's Privacy

LEX IQ is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform grows. We will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
