How It Started

The Story Behind LEX IQ

LEX IQ was built by a law student who was frustrated with how dry and inaccessible constitutional law study materials were. Reading bare acts is important — but there was no engaging way to test what you'd actually retained.

What started as a personal project to quiz a small batch of classmates quickly grew into something bigger. The live room feature was added so faculty could run real-time quizzes in the classroom. The leaderboard was added because competition motivates.

Today, LEX IQ is in trial mode — growing, improving, and shaped entirely by the feedback of its users. Every feature you see was built in response to a real need expressed by real law students.

We're just getting started.
